We study user's behaviors in order to help you create great products, apps and services

Methods

Observe, participate et make sense

Our field studies use methods from the social and human sciences. We do user research in order to understand what they do, how, and, if possible, why.

  • Ethnography (Participatory);
  • Audio and video recordings analysis;
  • Semi-structured interviews;
  • Questionnaires;
  • Card sorting.

Model and share

Parce que les projets réussissent dans la collaboration, nous modélisons nos découvertes avec des outils compréhensibles par tous.

  • Personas;
  • Scenarios;
  • User Journeys, Experience Maps.

Ideate, prototype and test

We use our findings and insights to create prototypes and test them with real users. Then we iterate and increase the fidelity of our prototypes to make them more interactive.

  • Wireframing & Prototyping (from lo-fi to hi-fi);
  • Usability Testing;
  • UX Measures.

We try to stay close to "real" situations (and avoid lab testing).
